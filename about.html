About Me

I’m a results-driven professional with over eight years of experience leading operations, people, and processes across various industries. My background blends creative direction, recruitment, systems development, and business operations — giving me a unique perspective on how to connect structure with innovation.

As an Operations Manager, I’ve led distributed teams, built internal systems from the ground up, and established standard operating procedures that improved workflow efficiency and performance visibility. I created multi-platform operational frameworks using tools like Notion, Google Workspace, and Discord, allowing for streamlined reporting, time tracking, and communication across multiple departments.

I’ve developed and implemented training and onboarding programs that ensure new hires integrate seamlessly into company workflows, reducing ramp-up time and increasing retention. These systems included structured progress tracking, one-on-one performance reviews, and clear performance metrics that aligned with company objectives.

Beyond team management, my expertise extends to process optimization and project oversight — from recruitment and client onboarding to data management and quality assurance. I take pride in transforming disorganized operations into organized, scalable systems that foster productivity, transparency, and measurable growth.

My leadership philosophy revolves around clarity, consistency, and collaboration. I believe great management doesn’t just keep things running — it builds systems that empower people to do their best work. Moving forward, I’m seeking opportunities where I can apply my experience in operations strategy, team leadership, and process improvement to help organizations reach new levels of efficiency and excellence.
